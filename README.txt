== Description ==

Give access to a contact form Gravity form on every page of your site as an avatar of a member of your team hung down the browser.
Personalize and humanize the navigation of your client in your content and media approach.

*Major features in Contact team member include:*

* Ability to change the sentences of the call to action , and to turn to vary over navigation.
* Personalize and humanize the navigation of your client in your content and media approach.
* Change the avatar displayed in the contact bubble.
* Associate the action click on the bubble to display a fomulaire Gravity Form to enable your users to contact you on all pages.
* Or, redirect users to click on the bubble to any internal or external page with a tracker to track the use of your contact bubble via Google Analytics.

PS: If you want the ability to display a form, you must install the plugin Gravity Form, http://www.gravityforms.com/.
